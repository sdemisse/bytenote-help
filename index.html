Byte Note: is an app where you can easily store random ideas appears to you in the middle of the day, quotes from a book you are reading, daily diary or events you have in the future. precisely with 99 words or less all in one place. You can go back and look them again anytime to remember your important idea, your next event or what a crazy day you had.

You can edit or delete your notes anytime by simply swiping right or left form the item list view
