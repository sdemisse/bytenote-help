Byte Drop: is a productive app where you can easily store random thoughts that appears to you in the middle of the day, quotes from a book you are reading, daily diary or events you have in the future. You can share these contents with your close friends who are in your contact list.

Your close friends who get your drop (note) can like your content, RVP the event you shared and write a comment.

You can go back and look at them again anytime to remember your important idea, your next event or what a crazy day you had.
